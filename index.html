Did you know?

The earliest use of candles is often atttibuted to the Ancient Egyptians, who made rushlights or torches by soaking the pithy core of reeds in melted animal fat. However, the rushlights had no wick like a true candle.

Today's Candles

Candles have come a long way since their initial use. While they are no longer used as a major source of light, they continue to grow in popularity and use.
